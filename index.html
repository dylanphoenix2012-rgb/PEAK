OUR STORY

Pleione Interactive is a new and emerging game-development studio in collaboration with other companies who are just as dedicated as us to crafting story-rich, immersive, and innovative video-game experiences. When we develop our games, we prioritize unique gameplay, mechanics, and compelling narratives over the overused modern-day tropes. We know you want something new, and we're here to deliver. Currently, we are working on our first game, "Ars Nova", which is a myth-centered game aiming to take stories from fan-favorite legends to even the most unknown folk tales. So I invite you, the passionate gamers, the enthusiasts, the lore-collectors, to join us, as we push new boundaries and forge new epics.
